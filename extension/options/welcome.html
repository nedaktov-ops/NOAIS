What NOAIS does

NOAIS scans every web page on-device for AI-generated text and shows you a colour-coded score. No network calls, no telemetry, no accounts — everything runs in your browser. Works on YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and any other site.

🎚️

Pick your mode

Soft mode (default) adds a small badge next to suspected AI content so you stay in control. Hard mode dims and blurs the content so you can scroll past without reading it. You can flip between modes per-site from the Settings page.

🧭

Tour the UI

Click the NOAIS icon in your toolbar to see the score for the current page. The Settings page lets you adjust the sensitivity slider, toggle sites on or off, and manage allowlisted elements.

🔒

Privacy promise

NOAIS makes zero network calls. It never sends the text of pages you visit anywhere. The only data stored is your settings, in your browser's local storage. See PRIVACY.md for the full disclosure.
